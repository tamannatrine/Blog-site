  • 250g cashew butter<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • 100g monk fruit erythritol sweetener<\/li>\n\n\n\n

      \n
    1. Preheat oven to 180\u00b0C \/ 355 \u00b0F.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
    3. Add all ingredients, except chocolate chips, to a large mixing bowl and mix until combined.<\/li>\n\n\n\n

    6. Transfer to oven and bake for 12 minutes or until golden brown.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
